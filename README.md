# EcoWise AI 🌱

## AI-Powered Carbon Footprint Tracking and Sustainability Recommendation System

### Overview

EcoWise AI is a web-based sustainability platform designed to help users understand and reduce their environmental impact. The system calculates annual carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions based on electricity consumption, travel habits, and country-specific energy generation data.

Unlike traditional carbon footprint calculators, EcoWise AI provides personalized sustainability recommendations and a Sustainability Score to encourage environmentally responsible behavior.

---

## Problem Statement

Many individuals are unaware of how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions and climate change. Existing solutions often present complex environmental data that is difficult for users to interpret.

EcoWise AI addresses this problem by providing a simple, interactive, and user-friendly platform that calculates carbon footprints and offers actionable recommendations for reducing emissions.

---

## Objectives

* Calculate annual carbon emissions based on user inputs.
* Analyze environmental impact using country-specific energy data.
* Generate sustainability recommendations.
* Provide a Sustainability Score to measure eco-friendliness.
* Promote awareness of climate change and responsible energy consumption.

---

## Key Features

### Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculates annual CO₂ emissions based on electricity consumption and user activity.

### Travel Impact Analysis

Considers daily travel distance while estimating carbon emissions.

### Sustainability Score

Generates a score out of 100 that reflects the user's environmental impact.

### AI Recommendations

Provides personalized suggestions to help users reduce their carbon footprint.

### Country-Based Analysis

Uses country-specific energy generation data for more realistic calculations.

## System Workflow

1. User selects a country.
2. User enters electricity consumption and travel distance.
3. System calculates annual CO₂ emissions.
4. Sustainability Score is generated.
5. AI Recommendation Engine analyzes the results.
6. User receives actionable sustainability suggestions.
